Case Study:

Golden Touch Jewelry

We gave Golden Touch Jewelry a complete e-commerce makeover, infusing luxury, streamlining checkout, and elevating their online presence to match the bold energy of their in-store experience.
Instagram Facebook Linkedin

Golden Touch Jewelry needed more than just a pretty website, they needed a digital storefront that matched the elegance of their pieces and made it easy for customers to buy.

The old site was dated, slow, and clunky on mobile. The brand didn’t feel luxurious. And the buying process? More frustrating than frictionless.

So we rebuilt it from the ground up.

Phase 1: Design with an Edge

We leaned into Western luxury meets high-end glam, a bold, brand-forward design using textured desert tones, warm golds, and dramatic typography.

  • Full-width hero section with a rotating product banner
  • Bold callouts to drive action: Sell Gold, Shop the Collection
  • Hand-selected feature area to showcase estate and designer pieces
  • Mobile-responsive layout for on-the-go shoppers

Every element was crafted to mirror the energy of their physical showroom, just online.

Phase 2: Streamlined Checkout Flow

What’s luxury if your checkout takes 7 clicks?

We simplified the buying journey by:

  • Integrating a streamlined cart system
  • Optimizing product pages for high-resolution visuals
  • Making the checkout flow clean, clear, and fast

Whether you’re selling vintage silver or high-karat gold, your site should feel as premium as the product. Now it does.

Phase 3: Built to Sell

We didn’t just design it to look good, we built it to convert.

We set up:

  • Conversion-focused buttons like “Call Now” and “Shop Now”
  • Clean navigation for services vs collections
  • A layout that keeps users scrolling, clicking, and exploring

The entire user journey is now built around engagement and action, from the second you land to the moment you purchase.

Results (and What’s Next)

Golden Touch Jewelry now has a sleek online experience to match their bold in-store presence.

Visitors are staying longer, clicking more, and buying directly from their site, especially on mobile. It’s the kind of upgrade that doesn’t just look better; it sells better.
